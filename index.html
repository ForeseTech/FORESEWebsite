    FORESE

    FORESE is a forum created for the sole purpose of equipping engineering students with team management, interpersonal and leadership skills. The forum is under the aegis of the Placement Cell.

    About FORESE
  • About FORESE

    FORESE and Mock Placements

    The Forum for Economic Studies by Engineers was created under the supervision of the Placement Cell of SVCE. The forum was created with an objective to furnish engineering students with the necessary skills to help them become industry-ready.
    The Mock Placements is an event organised by FORESE and has been its flasghip event since 2006. The event endeavours to prepare pre-final year students for the actual placements by letting HRs from various companies interview students. Students become aware of their shortcomings and strengths and can prepare accordingly. Previous versions of the event have seen hundreds of HRs and students participating.
